Roejlegaarden

Campsite: wagon and tents

No TV. No noise, apart from the sounds of sheep, chickens, bees, and cats.
10 minutes' bike ride along the fields to the sea.
500 meters to Østerbjerg station.
800 meters to the COOP, and eco-village shop.
Fireplace when it is not too windy

Wagon/hut with access to toilet and solar powered shower. Bring your own bed linen and utensils. Price per day: Dkr 200 kr.

The garden is spacious and a treat for garden lovers. It has several sections, most of which are colour-coded. Even though it can be windy around here, a calm spot can always be found. Find your own.

Our tent site is for cycklists, ramblers, and riders on horse back. We are part of the network www.teltpladser.dk. Price per person per night Dkr 30.
