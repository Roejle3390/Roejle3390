Roejlegaarden
- more than just a farm

Røjlegården is a classical four-winged Danish farm with a cobbled stone yard, and a delightful garden. We run an organic farm with sheep, bees, hens - and cats. Our farm has no direct neighbours: space for peace and thinking. Very quiet. It is situated centrally on sunny Halsnæs, a peninsula with sticks out into the Kattegat, with the sea to the North and the fjord to the South.

Our large garden is special …a place for garden enthusiasts. We have devided it into many parts: the mossy part, the warm corner, the herbal garden. All parts have a particular colour scheme. New sections are on their way, one is close by the willow hut. Take your time to discover this mavellous garden. Not-so-windy corners are always to be found. Find one for yourself.

We take pride in being as self-sufficient as possible in terms of our kitchen gardens and our orchard. This philosophy is reflected in our solar panels which produce warm water and electricity enough to cover our needs for power and heating. And for the rest we cut & chop our own wood for the stoves. We don't mind showing you these wonders.

Our farm has a blacksmith of the most classical sort, doing museum work, and a yoga centre. We form an active part of Torup Booktown: we are in fact the intiators of introducing this wonderful rural 2nd hand bookshop concept in Denmark.

A newly decorated B&B room with kitchen facilities and solar-powered shower is found in the Eastwing on the first floor. Up a rather steep staircase. In addition our converted barn is now a space which we use as a workshed, or for concerts, or Book Town events. See Links for more information.

Location?

Røjlegården is situated in North Sjælland just outside Torup village between Hundested and Frederiksværk.

Train from København: S-train to Hillerød; change for Frederiksværkbanen to Hundested.
Train every ½ hour. Press stop before Østerbjerg. Bring your bicykle on the train.

The stop before Østerbjerg is the new restored Dyssekilde Station with local food, i.e. a farmers' market every second Saturday during the Summer.
Dyssekilde Station is also the headquarter of Torup Booktown: take your time to browse to books.
